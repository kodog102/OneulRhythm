# OneulRhythm Product Principles

## 1. Today's rhythm is more important than today's tasks.

The application exists to help users stay connected with today's rhythm.

---

## 2. Less Input. More Presence.

Users should spend less time managing the application.

The application should quietly remain present throughout the day.

---

## 3. Calm over Complexity.

Reduce decisions.

Reduce visual noise.

Reduce configuration.

---

## 4. One Primary Focus.

Only one rhythm deserves primary attention.

The application decides which rhythm matters most.

---

## 5. Consistency Across Surfaces.

Today Screen

Live Activity

Notifications

Widgets

Apple Watch

All surfaces consume the same schedule.

None of them define scheduling independently.

---

## 6. Simplicity Before Power.

The MVP should solve the common case.

Advanced customization should be introduced only after validating real user demand.

---

## 7. Architecture Serves Experience.

Technology is not the product.

A simple and calm experience is the product.
